About Cannabis Madrid

Our story, mission, and commitment to the Madrid cannabis community

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Cannabis Madrid began as a small passion project with a big vision: to provide Madrid's cannabis community with access to premium products and expert guidance in a safe, welcoming environment.

What started as a dream between friends who shared a love for cannabis culture has grown into Madrid's premier dispensary, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the city.

Located in the heart of Gran Vía, we've become a cornerstone of Madrid's cannabis community, known for our commitment to quality, education, and responsible cannabis use.

Growing Since 2019

Our Mission

To provide Madrid's cannabis community with premium products, expert guidance, and a safe space for education and exploration. We believe in the power of cannabis to enhance lives when used responsibly and with proper knowledge.

Quality First

We source only the highest quality products from trusted local growers and suppliers.

Education

We believe in empowering our customers with knowledge about cannabis and responsible use.

Community

We're committed to building a strong, supportive cannabis community in Madrid.

Safety

All our products are lab-tested and we promote safe, responsible cannabis consumption.
